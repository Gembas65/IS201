Experience & Achivements

The more I play and practice, the more experience I gain.

I have played golf for over 50 years and have had the chance to play on all continents, compete, and hold cards on many first-class tours.

  1. Amateur Achievements
    • Colombian junior champion 1980, 1981, and 1982
    • Junior world Runner-up
    • World Amateur individual Champion 1986
    • Three times All-American Division I
    • WAC player of the year 1987
    • Six College tournament victories
  2. Professional Regular Tour Achievements
    • Ranked in the top 100 players in the world in 1999
    • JGT (Japan Golf Tour) member 1988~2002
    • Six victories in the JGT
    • Member of the PGA Tour 2002
    • Member of the Nationwide Tour 2003~2009
    • Mexican Open Champion 2003
    • Winner of over 20 professional golf tournaments worldwide
    • World cup team participant 1994~1997
    • Three times British Open participant
  3. Professional Champions Tour Achievements
    • Two times Florida Senior Open Champion 2015 and 2017
    • Two times US Open Senior participant
  4. Other Golf Achievements
    • First Colombian in the top 100 of the GWR
    • The Colombia National Junior Championship carries my name after my win in the World Amateur Championship in 1986
    • Two times captain of the Latin-American Team
    • Coauthor with Phil Ritson of the “Teach the Teacher Project.”
