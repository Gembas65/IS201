Education

This is what I've learned.

Learning has been challenging and, at the same time, fun! In my early days, I remember the struggles when I arrived at Brigham Young University on a golf scholarship with little knowledge of the English language. Then after completing school, I moved to Japan to start my professional career on the Japanese Tour, again a new culture and a new language. Golf has been the bridge that allowed me to adjust to all these changes in my life. My passion for the game has the key to opening my mind to learn from all different people and cultures. I've been lucky to have had excellent teachers!
